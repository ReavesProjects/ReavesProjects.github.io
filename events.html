EDUCATION

Our focus is on educating potential and future adopters on cat behavior and care. Our classes are free and open to all in a small group envinroment. Guests should feel safe to ask questions and share stories and challenges.

COMMUNITY

We partner with local rescue groups, elder centers and local schools. We welcome charity groups to respectfully socialize with our cats under supervision with trained cat behaviorists.

ONGOING

Finni's offers cultural programming to benefit NYC cats and potential adopters. We welcome you to join or contact us to suggest pertinent and substantive programs that encourage adoption, healing and community.
