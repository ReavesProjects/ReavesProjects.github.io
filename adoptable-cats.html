GEORGE CLUNY

Eleven Year Old Male - American Short-Hair (Deaf)

Yes, of course I named him. No other name could properly pay tribute to this dapper, grey-haired, gorgeous gentlman. George Cluny is a total love bug who likes to snuggle.
