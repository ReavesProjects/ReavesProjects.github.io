FEATURED CAT

GEORGE CLUNY

Eleven Year Old Male - American Short-Hair

Yes, I named him. No other name could properly pay tribute to this dapper, grey-haired, gentlman. George Cluny is a total love bug. He is very mellow so if you are looking for a playful kitty, he is not your man but he will keep your secrets and adore every moment together.

While he appears to be a Scottish Fold, his ears have this appearance due to an ear infection that was not treated during his time as a stray. We believe him to be mostly if not completely deaf. He is also on a special diet and requires a bit more attention from the vet based on age. He is Neutured, up to date on all shots and microchipped.
