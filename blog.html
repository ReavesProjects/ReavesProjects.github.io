THE MYTH OF THE BLACK CAT

It's Not Just About Halloween, Nov 2, 2018


Black cats are perhaps the most misundertood of all pets. Their mystique is homage to countless Halloween myths and decorations but black cats make great pets 365 days a year.

Often overlooked by their other color counterparts because they are hard to photograph, black cats (and dogs) have the highest rate of euthansia. They are not bad luck but this fact does make them unlucky in areas where high-kill shelters are prominent.

We invite you to meet our adorable, adoptable cats of all colors. We believe love is colorblind.

ADOPTING A SENIOR CAT

Older, Wiser and Most At Risk, Aug 11, 2018


Adopting a pet is a huge commitment. While a cat does not have 9 lives, with proper diet and medical care, they can live past 9 years, some celebrating up to 20.

Like their human counterparts, medical issues arise as pets get older. Vet visits become more frequent, dietary needs may change and - though cats never lose their hunter instincts - you may see signs of slowing.

The good news for humans considering seniors is the ability to truly make a difference and save a life. Sure kittens are cute but older cats don't feel the need to destroy your couch, throw 3am disco parties or climb your new curtains, instead they opt for belly rubs, a warm spot of sunlight and the chance to spend the rest of their lives safe with you.
